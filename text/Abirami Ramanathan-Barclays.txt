• Some Tips  
- 
Be confident while answering the questions. 
- 
Be truthful to your resume. You must be thorough with every nook and 
corner of your resume, especially your projects. 
- 
Have a habit of solving DSA problems, and revising DBMS / OOPS concepts 
regularly.  
- 
In Barclays interviews many were asked on concepts like ER Diagram, 
Normalization, etc. 
- 
Try to maintain a CGPA of about 8+ , if otherwise, focus on other projects to 
compensate. 
- 
Read the job description and you must do a research about the company.  
- 
 Refer to the company's website to learn about RISES.
