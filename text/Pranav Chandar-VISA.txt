 
 
 
   Visa Internship Offer 
About the company: 
Visa Inc. is an American Multinational payments technology company which aims in providing 
fast, reliable and secure digital payments all around the world. It is often misinterpreted as a bank or 
credit card company. But actually, it provides financial services and facilitates digital payments.  
There were 2 rounds of selection process. One was an Online test and another was a Technical 
cum Managerial interview. 
1. Online test: 
It was conducted on hackerRank platform for 60 minutes. There were 2 coding questions of medium 
level difficulty. 
• 
First question was from Graphs. The core logic of the question was to find the number of nodes 
in each connected component of a Disconnected graph. 
https://leetcode.com/discuss/interview-question/connected-sum-in-a-graph 
• 
Second question was from Arrays. Given an integer array and an integer k, count all the distinct 
pairs in that array whose difference is equal to ‘k’. 
https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/count-pairs-difference-equal-k/ 
Since there were only 2 questions, we were expected to solve both. A total of 17 students were 
shortlisted for the next round. 
2. Technical cum Managerial interview: 
It was conducted in MS teams. The duration of the interview was around 1 hour. Initially he asked me to 
introduce myself.  
First half of the interview was fully technical. 
• 
He started with my project on Blockchain and Cryptocurrency (since Visa is all about payments 
and transactions, he was very interested in that project). 
• 
He then asked me to brief my DBMS project. There were some follow up questions on that: 
o What is Normalization and why is it used ? 
o What are the security features that you have implemented in your project ? 
o What were the challenges faced by you during the course of project ? 
• 
After that he asked me some basic questions on DSA like: 
o What is main difference between array and stack ? 
o What is the difference between void and null (in programming languages) ? 
• 
Then we went on to the pair coding screen in hackerRank platform and he gave me a coding 
question. 
o https://leetcode.com/problems/jump-game-vi/ 
o I explained my O(n2) approach to him. 
o He asked me whether I know about Segment tree data structure. Since I said No, he told 
me that my approach can further be optimized by storing the calculated values in 
Segment tree. 
And rest of the interview was managerial. There were some usual HR questions like: 
• 
Tell me an incident that highlights your leadership and teamwork skills (since I have put both in 
my Resume). 
• 
How have you helped others in your college ? 
• 
Tell me a situation where you have been so stressful in your life and how did you overcome it ? 
• 
Tell me an instance where you have worked sincerely to meet a deadline. 
• 
How will be your learning curve when you want to learn something new (especially online) ? 
• 
How will you approach a new project given to you (along with your team) ? 
Finally, he asked me whether I had any questions for him. I asked him a couple of questions. Interview 
was over then. 
 
Key takeaways from interview experience: 
• 
Read & re-read your Resume. Be very clear about every single line put in your Resume. 
• 
Do an intensive research on the company profile and try to understand how do they make 
money out of their business. 
• 
Joining the interview 5 mins prior to the scheduled time will be very helpful. 
• 
Also, we can have a small conversation with the interviewer to get connected well. 
• 
Interviewers are not here just to reject us and they will definitely help us when we get struck 
somewhere. 
• 
Don’t lose your hope in-between the interview, if you answer wrong to 1 or 2 questions. 
 
Topics to be focused: 
• 
Data Structures and Algorithms. 
• 
Database Management Systems. 
• 
OOPS. 
• 
Security aspect in whatever skills and projects mentioned.
