There were 3 rounds in the selection process. 
 
Round 1: 
 
This round was conducted online which included aptitude, verbal, logical reasoning 
and two coding questions. One coding question was related to strings and the other one was 
from matrix which involved transposal and similar fundamental operations of matrix like 
matrix multiplication. 
 
Round 2: 
1) Introduce yourself 
 
2) Program to find the sum of the digits. 
 
3) OOPS concepts with real-life examples 
 
4) Detailed explanations of projects.(They will give some scenarios and ask how you will 
solve it?) 
 
5) Newspaper puzzle 
 
6)Why citi? 
 
7)Questions if any? 
 
HR :
