     VISA INTERNSHIP OFFER 
  ABOUT VISA, 
Visa Inc. is an American Multinational payments technology company which aims in providing 
fast, reliable and secure digital payments all around the world. It is often misinterpreted as a bank or 
credit card company. But actually, it provides financial services and facilitates digital payments. 
There were 2 rounds of selection process. One was an Online test and another was a Technical 
cum Managerial interview. 
1. Online test: 
It was conducted on hackerRank platform for 70 minutes. There were 4 coding questions of 2 Easy level 
difficulty and 2 medium level difficulty. 
• 
First Question: Given an array of n integers, write a function that returns the index of the first 
element in the array such that the next two consecutive elements (i.e., the element at the 
current index and the next two elements) are strictly greater than a given threshold. If no such 
index exists, return -1. 
• 
Second Question: You are given an array numbers. Distribute its elements between two arrays, 
first and second, as follows: 
1. The first element goes to first, the second to second. 
2. For each subsequent element, if the number of elements strictly greater than the 
current element is higher in one array, place the element in the other. If both have the 
same count, place the element in the shorter array. If still tied, place it in first. 
3. Return the merged result of first followed by second. 
• 
Third Question: You are given an n x m grid and an array figures representing different Tetris-
like shapes (A, B, C, D, E). Starting with a matrix of zeros, your task is to draw each figure on 
the grid using its 1-based index from the array. The shapes are placed on the grid according to 
specific patterns. Return the resulting matrix after placing all the shapes. 
• 
Fourth Question: Given an array of integers arr, write a function to count how many 
contiguous subarrays form a sawtooth sequence, where each element alternates in parity 
(even or odd) from its neighbors. 
Since there were only 4 questions, we were expected to solve both. A total of 15 students were 
shortlisted for the next round. 
2. Technical cum Managerial interview: 
It was conducted in MS teams. The duration of the interview was around 1 hour. Initially he asked me to 
introduce myself. 
There were some usual HR questions like: 
• 
Can you share a specific experience where you faced a significant challenge? How did you 
overcome it, and what important lesson did you take away from the situation? 
• 
What do you consider to be your weakness? 
• 
Tell me a situation where you have been so stressful in your life and how did you overcome it? 
• 
How would you handle a project with limited resources and a tight deadline? 
• 
Can you describe a situation where you received no initial instructions and explain how you 
managed to successfully navigate it? 
Finally, he asked me whether I had any questions for him. I asked him a couple of questions. 
• 
What does the work culture at Visa look like? 
• 
What are some common challenges interns might encounter during their internship? 
• 
What is the primary responsibility of an intern during the internship process? 
• 
I inquired about accommodation options during the internship. 
After the interview concluded, the results were announced the next day. Out of 15 
candidates, 5 were selected, and I’m one of them 
Key takeaways from interview experience: 
• 
Read & re-read your Resume. Be very clear about every single line put in your Resume. 
• 
Do an intensive research on the company profile and try to understand how do they make 
money out of their business. 
• 
Joining the interview 5 mins prior to the scheduled time will be very helpful. 
• 
Also, we can have a small conversation with the interviewer to get connected well. 
• 
Interviewers are not here just to reject us and they will definitely help us when we get struck 
somewhere. 
• 
Don’t lose your hope in-between the interview, if you answer wrong to 1 or 2 questions.
