1. Online Test 
The assessment was conducted on the Codility platform and included 3 problem-
solving questions: 
1. Subarray Maximum Sum Problem – From a given integer array, find a subarray 
that both starts and ends with the same element, and calculate the maximum 
possible sum among such subarrays. 
2. Garbage Truck Scheduling – A row of houses has different types of waste 
(Metal, Plastic, Glass). Separate trucks are assigned to each waste type. Each 
truck travels to the farthest house of its type, collects the waste (taking 1 
minute per bag), and returns. The task was to compute the overall time taken 
(maximum among all trucks). 
3. Grid Navigation with Constraints – An assassin has to move from a starting 
cell to the bottom-right corner of an n × m grid. Guards monitor straight lines 
in their direction until blocked, and all cells under their vision are unsafe. 
Obstacles are also present. The task was to determine if the assassin can safely 
reach the target cell under these conditions. 
2. Interview 
The interviews were conducted in virtual mode and had two rounds. 
i) Technical Interview 
Duration: 30 - 35 minutes 
The round started with a self-introduction followed by a detailed discussion on my 
projects. The interviewer asked many questions about: 
• 
The tech stack I used 
• 
The reasoning behind choosing those technologies 
• 
Design choices and scalability 
One problem I was asked: 
  A file contains dates. How would you sort it? 
Since I had recently completed a related course on Coursera, I confidently explained 
the solution using Pandas (read_csv) and Python datetime functions, describing the 
complete workflow. The interviewer was satisfied with my explanation. 
Finally, I was asked if I had any questions. I inquired about how freshers feel when 
they first join Amex and about the work culture. 
ii) HR Interview 
Duration: 45 minutes 
This round was largely focused on my projects, behavior, and communication. 
The interviewer asked me to explain one project from idea to completion. I chose my 
Data Science Capstone Project – Success Prediction of Falcon 9 Rocket Launches 
 . I described the source of the idea, the dataset, methodology, and outcomes. 
Since it was unique compared to common projects, it created a positive impression. 
 
 
Other key areas of discussion: 
• 
Challenges I faced during projects 
• 
How quickly I can adapt to new languages/technologies 
• 
Handling pressure and managing time effectively 
• 
My interests, favorite subject, and future learning goals 
Similar to the technical round, I ended by asking about company culture and fresher 
experience at Amex. 
TIPS 
  Be clear and confident while explaining your projects, as they form the core of 
the discussion. 
  If you don’t know something, it’s okay to admit it – show your willingness to 
learn. 
  Prepare for scenario-based questions with real-life examples (challenges, 
teamwork, achievements, failures). 
  Research the company and role beforehand; it makes your answers more aligned. 
  Maintain a positive attitude and sound confident throughout.
