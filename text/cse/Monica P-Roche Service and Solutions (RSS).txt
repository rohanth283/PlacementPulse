RECRUITMENT PROCESS: 
1. Online Test 
2. Case Study 
3. Group Discussion 
4. Techno managerial round 
5. Techno HR Round 
Online Test: 
• 
3 Sections - MCQ 
• 
Section 1: Aptitude, English, Logical Reasoning 
• 
Section 2: Pandas, SQL query, Excel 
• 
Section 3:SQL Query   
•

Case study: 
•

• 
Asked 5 questions with each carrying marks like 5,10,15. 
• 
We are asked to write solution for the questions (analytics kind of questions) 
•

•

Group Discussion: 
•

•

•

OVERALL EXPERIENCE: 
1. They tested communication skills, basic skill 
2. More importantly they tested logical reasoning, how fast your brain tries to give the optimal 
solution 
3. For that I suggest solving problems on leetcode and trying to solve it, it basically increases 
the problemsolving skills 
4. When u read basic cs core concepts, make a flashcard or quick revision notes 
5. So you can go thru It whenever needed 
6. You should be learning dsa+communication+core cs for your placements
