KLA TENCOR 
KLA develops industry-leading equipment and services that enable innovation 
throughout the electronics industry.  They provide advanced process control and 
process-enabling solutions for manufacturing wafers and reticles, integrated 
circuits, packaging, printed circuit boards and flat panel displays. 
 
 
Total no. of rounds: 5 (Online Test, Technical Interview I, Technical Interview II, 
Managerial Interview, HR Interview) 
Eligibility Criteria: CGPA 7.5 and above 
CTC : 18 LPA 
Job Role Offered: Software Engineer 
 
Round 1: Online Test 
• Platform: HackerRank 
• Difficulty level: Easy 
• Duration: 90 minutes 
• Number of Questions: 2 
• Question 1: Smallest Negative Balance 
• Question 2: A slight variation of Sort Roman Numerals 
25 people were shortlisted for the technical interview 
 
 
Round 2: Technical Interview I 
• Platform: HackerRank Codepair 
• Difficulty level: Medium 
• Duration: 60 minutes 
The shortlisted candidates were given codepair links to attend the technical 
interview.  The interviewer asked to give a short introduction about myself.  Make 
sure you prepare a nice and crisp introduction with a maximum of 1.30 – 2 mins.  
After that, I was given 2 coding questions to be solved in the CodePair IDE (1 Easy 
and 1 Medium).  Don’t just focus on typing your code, explain your thought 
process throughout and make sure that you get your interviewer understand your 
train of thought, so that even if you are stuck somewhere, the interviewer could 
give the required hints.  Also, don’t get nervous whenever you get stuck 
somewhere, always be bold and confident. 
Coding Questions: 
• Divisibility of Strings 
• Sum of all parts of square matrix after divided by its diagonals 
13 people were shortlisted for the next round 
Round 3: Technical Interview II 
• Platform: HackerRank Codepair 
• Difficulty level: Medium 
• Duration: 60 minutes 
This round focused mainly on problem solving skills of the candidate.  I was asked 
to explain the approach and write pseudo-code for 2 given problem statements (1 
Easy and 1 Medium).  Make sure you communicate as much as possible, it always 
plays to your advantage.  Time complexities of each of the solutions were asked. 
Questions: 
• Convert an infix string to postfix, and evaluate the postfix expression. 
• Given ‘N’ processes (execution time for each process is given in seconds) 
and ‘C’ cpu cores, where a process could depend on zero or more 
processes and compiles only after all the process it depends on finishes 
executing (no cyclic dependency).  Schedule the processes and print the 
state of all the cores after each second. 
6 people were shortlisted for the next round 
Round 4: Managerial Interview 
• Platform: Zoom 
• Difficulty Level: Medium-Hard 
• Duration: 60 minutes 
This round was taken by a Senior Manager at KLA.  It mainly focused on system 
design with some HR questions.  I was asked to design an application that would 
take a script as input and execute it.  There was no code involved, only the 
classes, and their corresponding responsibilities were asked, along with the usage 
of appropriate data structures used in each of the functionalities in depth.  Some 
basic HR questions were asked in the end. 
 
Round 5: HR Interview 
• Platform: Zoom 
• Duration: 25 minutes 
Started off with basic HR questions, but the interviewer started questioning based 
on answers given to the previous questions.  Make sure you research about the 
company well and prepare for HR questions beforehand so that you aren’t caught 
off guard by any question. 
4 of us were selected as FTE at KLA Tencor, Chennai. 
Tips: 
• It is highly recommended to restrict your resume to only one page and 
make sure it is devoid of any spelling/grammatical errors. 
• While practising your DSA, give contests regularly in coding sites (Leetcode, 
CodeChef, GeeksforGeeks, etc.) 
• Research the company well.  
• Go through previous year interview experiences from geeksforgeeks. 
• Its ok to start late, now is the best time to start preparing!
