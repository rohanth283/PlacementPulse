 
 
 
 
 
   VERIZON 
 
 
Verizon is an American multinational telecommunications organization which specializes in 
providing wireless products and services. 
 
The interview process consisted of three rounds. 
 
Round 1: MCQs from Aptitude, Verbal, Technical and two coding problems. 
 
I got most of the MCQs right. I solved one problem fully and the other one partially. 
 
Round 2: Interviews - It consisted of 2 Panels (Technical and HR)  
 
 
Technical Round: 
 
1. Tell me about yourself. 
 
2. Explain about your projects. 
 
3. What were the difficulties you faced when working on your projects? 
 
4. Which domain in computer science are you interested in? 
 
    I told Databases and he asked a few questions in it. 
 
5. How much understanding you have about AI and ML. 
 
    I told him I only had a brief idea about them and not in detail. 
 
6. Anything you would like to ask me? 
 
    I asked about Verizon’s 5G tech and its progress. 
 
 
Overall Interview Time: 30 mins 
 
 
HR Round: 
 
1. Tell me about yourself. 
 
2. Why work and not higher studies? I told I could gain more experience and knowledge about 
latest tech only when working. 
 
3. Will you be comfortable if asked to reallocate to another city? I said yes. 
 
4. Will you be comfortable working in shifts? I said yes. 
 
5. Anything you would like to ask me? 
 
   Overall Interview Time: 15 mins 
Topics to be focussed: 
 
 Data Structures 
 Database Management Systems 
 Operating System 
 
 
Take away: 
 
 
The whole Interview process was smooth. Being strong in one programming knowledge and 
having a fair knowledge about core computer science concepts is well and good to get placed in this 
company.
