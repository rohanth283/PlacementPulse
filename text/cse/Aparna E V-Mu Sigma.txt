Mu Sigma 
 
Mu Sigma is an Indian decision sciences firm that primarily offers data analytics services. 
The firm's name is derived from the statistical terms "Mu" and "Sigma" which symbolize the 
mean and the standard deviation, respectively, of a probability distribution. 
It consists of two rounds: 
Round 1: Aptitude. 
To clear this round, we need a basic knowledge of quantitative aptitude and logical reasoning. 
Since I have already practiced enough aptitude questions for placement, this aptitude round 
was pretty easy to solve. 
Aptitude is important for most service-based companies like TCS, Infosys, Wipro, etc. So 
practice enough aptitude questions in IndiaBix. 
Round 2: Interview through Video-Recording. 
This round consists of mostly analytics questions. We need to analyze and answer the 
questions firmly with normal English fluency. 
That is enough for mu sigma recruitment. 
But for other product-based companies, practice many coding questions as much as you can 
in geeks for geeks and Hackerrank. And be very clear in OOPS and DBMS concepts for the 
technical interview. Whatever you study be clear in those concepts. For the HR round, 
boldness is the key. Correct or wrong say with confidence. 
The most important thing is don’t ever lose your hope during the placement period. You will 
surely be get placed in one of the companies. So work hard, be confident and wait for your 
time.  
That’s all about my Placement experience ☺.
