Morgan Stanley (Internship) 
 
About the company: 
Morgan Stanley is an American multinational investment bank and financial services 
company. Morgan Stanley mobilizes capital to help governments, corporations, institutions and 
individuals around the world achieve their financial goals. 
 
Round 1: Online Coding Round 
 
This round consisted of MCQs and 3 coding questions. The MCQs included aptitude, logical 
and technical questions each in different sections. The coding questions were based on some of the 
following topics strings, DP and graph and the difficulty level of those three questions were easy, 
medium and hard respectively. 
 
Round 2: Technical Round 
 
This round was for about 40 mins. Mainly asked standard questions in OS, DBMS, Data 
Structures and OOPS. Here are some of the questions I remember. 
• Tail recursion 
• Explain red black tree and AVL tree and state the difference 
• Merge two Linked list 
• Topological sorting 
It would be sufficient, if you had enough knowledge in core subjects and practiced well in top 
coding websites. 
 
Round 3: HR Round 
 
This round was for about 45 mins. Asked common HR questions and a design question. 
• Tell me about yourself 
• Explain about your leadership skills. 
• Asked to explain one of my projects and my role in that project. 
• Whether faced any issues while developing, if any what are those issues and how you 
resolved it. 
• Hardest decision you made. 
• Asked to draw class diagram for movie ticket booking system. 
Answer each question boldly, don’t hesitate for anything, speak with confidence. 
 
Topics to be focused: 
• Data structures and algorithm 
• Key knowledge in at least one programming language. 
• OOPS, OS and DBMS. 
 
 
 
 
Take away 
• Practice well in coding platforms. 
• Have sufficient knowledge in core subjects. 
• Be confident while you speak to the interviewer.
