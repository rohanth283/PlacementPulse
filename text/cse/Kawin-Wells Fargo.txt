Wells Fargo 
Wells Fargo & Company is an American multinational financial services company. It 
is the fourth largest bank in the US by total assets. Wells Fargo is ranked number 26 on the 
2018 Fortune 500 rankings of the largest US corporations by total revenue. 
Round 1 : Online Aptitude Test (Amcat) 
 
The test consisted of Logical, English, Stock market graph analysis sections. The last 
section was coding which had 2 problems. 
1. A Dynamic Programming problem based on Maximum Length Chain of Pairs. 
2. A Graph problem based on Maximum Flow. 
 
Round 2 :  Technical Interview - 1 
 
This round went for about 45 mins. Technical questions were asked from  OOPS , OS 
and Networks concepts. Some puzzle question were also asked. The interviewer asked me to 
code Binary Search program and gave some test cases. Algorithms for Quick sort, Finding 
the kth smallest element in an array were also asked. 
 
Round 3 : Technical Interview – 2 
 
In this round, the interviewer asked me to explain one of my projects which I felt was 
very challenging and also asked how to solve some loopholes in the project. Then, I was 
asked how to implement a Chess game and how to detect a Checkmate. The final question 
was how Uber sets the route for the driver to the customer’s pickup point. 
 
HR round : 
 
HR round was very casual. They asked me to ask  if I have any questions. I asked 
some questions about the company goals, their work and experience etc.  
 
Topics to focus : 
1. Problem solving and Algorithms 
2. OOPS, DBMS, OS & Networks 
3. System Design 
I hope this will be helpful for those attending the interviews in this company. All the best !
