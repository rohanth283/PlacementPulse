 
 
 
 
 
TEMENOS 
 
Temenos is a company specialising in enterprise software for banks and financial 
services, with its headquarters in Geneva, Switzerland. It  was initially created in 1993, and 
has been listed on the Swiss stock exchange since 2001. It serves over 3,000 financial 
institutions in 145 countries across the world. It claims to be used by 41 of the top 50 banks 
worldwide.
