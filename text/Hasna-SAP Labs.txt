 
 
 
 
 
 
SAP LABS 
 
 
  
Rounds and domains for the interns selection. 
 
 
Round 1: Technical & coding questions 
 
Consisted of a select number of technical questions and two coding questions of varying difficulty 
on OOPS concepts, Data Structures like arrays, trees, linked lists and also focussed on DBMS. 
 
Round 2: It consists of 3 Panels(Technical 1,Technical 2, HR)  
 
Technical Round 1: 
 
1. Introduce yourself? 
 
2. Gave a scenario e.g., Student teacher online assessment system and asked to draw an ER diagram 
on it and asked questions based on what I drew. Then asked me to write queries [Keen on small 
details ]. 
 
3. Gave 3-4 questions on arrays and linked list after he made a note of what languages I was good 
in. 
 
Overall he focussed mainly on basic technical concepts and quizzed on them. 
 
 
Technical Round 2: 
 
In this round, the interviewer focussed on what projects I did and the domain I was interested in 
exploring. He then asked me basic questions on machine learning[what I said], and then 2-3 
technical questions to solve[with  and questions like what data structure would be suitable for 
different scenarios and how it is currently implemented and how we can upgrade that. 
 
 
HR: 
 
The HR round was very casual as compared to other rounds. They are just trying to get to know 
more about yourself and also wanted to have a slight idea about our communication and team skills. 
 
1.Why Sap Labs? 
 
2. About the relocation(yes/no)? I said Yes 
 
3. Hobbies, personal interests, etc. 
 
4. How to work in harmony with the company etc. 
 
5.Team building skills etc. 
 
 
 
Topics to be focussed: 
 
◆ Data structures 
◆ Database management systems 
◆ Basic Concepts like OOPS, pointers etc. 
 
 
Take away: 
 
 
Having a good knowledge of basics, coding and confidence will help to be placed in this 
company.
