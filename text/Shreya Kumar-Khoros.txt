 
 
 
 
 
 
KHOROS 
 
 
Khoros is a global customer engagement software company that provides online community 
management, social media marketing, social media analytics, digital care, and content management 
software and services to enterprise brands and agencies. 
 
Totally, there were 4 rounds.  
 
Round 1: 
 
We were given 40 logical questions and had to solve those questions in 20 minutes. Depending on 
how many you answer correctly, you will be shortlisted. 20 students were shortlisted after this 
round. 
 
Round 2 (Technical Round): 
 
I was asked to code (white boarding) 2 questions and they were of moderate level. 
1) Delete Duplicate numbers from a linked list. 
2) The same question as above, but with slight modification. 
The interviewer might see how your approach to the problem is, your code need not be entirely 
correct. My interviewer gave me some ideas while I was coding to see how I would handle them. 
 
Round 3 (Managerial Round): 
 
I was asked some questions based on my resume. 
1) To explain the most challenging project from my resume. 
2) Scope of my Final Year Project. 
3) How I would manage a team. 
..and a few other basic questions. 
 
Round 4 (HR Round): 
 
HR round was very basic and the interviewer wanted to see how we interacted. General 
questions like “Why should we hire you?”, “Why Khoros?”, etc. were asked. 
After all the rounds, 4 people were selected and I was one among them. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Topics to be focussed: 
 
 Data structures 
 
 
Take away: 
 
 
Be strong in your data structures concepts. Almost everyone were asked questions related to 
data structures only.
