 
 
 
Enphase Energy – Bangalore, India. 
Role: Associate Software Engineer – DBU  
Number of rounds: 
 
Enphase Energy conducted a one-day campus drive where they had 
3 rounds of interviews (1 Technical + 2 HR Rounds).  
Technical Round: 
 
Following the Resume Screening Process, I was shortlisted for the 
Technical round where I was given coding exercises and was asked 
questions on OOPS Concepts, Data Structures, DB, and Web/Mobile App 
Development. 
HR: 
 
Subsequently, I was shortlisted for the next round with the HR. This 
round completely revolved around my resume and the projects I had done 
during college. Finally, I had a one-on-one session with the Vice President 
and Managing Director of Enphase Energy - India, where he talked about 
my role at the company, and also gave me an insight into the company’s 
work culture and campus life. 
 
After 2 days when I finally got my offer, I was super elated. The 
placement experience on the whole was truly exhilarating and I am 
looking forward to working with the team.
