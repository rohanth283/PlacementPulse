Never lie in your resume

generating questions based on it. Meanwhile, I gave my self-introduction.

HR Round

Key Takeaways 
• Never lie in your resume — be prepared for any question based on it. 
• DSA + DBMS + OOPS are must-prepare topics. 
• Be ready to explain every project you mention. 
• Have knowledge of API concepts and real-world scenarios. 
• Solve LeetCode problems regularly — they may appear with slight 
modifications. 
• CGPA plays a vital role in the shortlisting process. 
My tips to juniors…. 
Prepare well. 
When asked "Tell me about yourself", speak clearly and confidently. Also, 
know about the company — their goals and the new steps they are taking. 
While solving DSA problems, keep speaking continuously. Talk about your 
approach, explain whatever you are thinking, and share what is running through 
your mind about the problem. Don’t let the interviewer search for the next 
question — keep the conversation active. 
Highlight the novelty of your project, its purpose, and the target consumers of 
your project. Have these points at your fingertips.
