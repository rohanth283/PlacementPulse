INTERVIEW EXPERIENCE

Company: Barclays

During my interview, I was asked questions on various topics, including:

1.  OOP Concepts: Be prepared to discuss object-oriented programming principles such

as inheritance, polymorphism, encapsulation, and abstraction.

2.  Authentication and Authorization: Understand the difference between these two
concepts, how they are implemented, and common security practices related to
them.

3.  Cloud Computing: I was asked what is cloud and cloud service models (IaaS, PaaS,

SaaS)

Additionally, the interviewer asked me to explain the project I had listed on my resume. It’s
essential to be clear about the project's scope, your role, the technologies used, and the
outcomes.

4.  Company Values (RISES): I was also asked about the company's values, referred to as
RISES. You should be familiar with these values (you can look them up online) and
think of a real-life incident where you demonstrated one or more of these values. Be
ready to share this incident and explain how it aligns with the company's core values.
