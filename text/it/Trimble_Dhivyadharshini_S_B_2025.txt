**Placement Experience – TRIMBLE**

* **Online test:**
* 2 coding questions and 5 mcqs
* Coding questions were based on string concepts and mcqs was about aptitude, SQL
* Test was for one hour
* **Interview:**
* **First round:**

Formal introduction and a walk through of your profile.

Since TRIMBLE is a hardware and software based company, generally in every round interviewers were very much interested in my IOT project( wearable for women and child safety with an APP to control)

And after this , the real interview started, there were questions from OOPS( design necessary classes objects for library management system

* To add a book with title, author, ISBN no
* To withdraw a book based on author
* To search for a based on ISBN no)

DSA( to find the sum of all nodes in BST)

PUZZLES( reasoning questions, math based questions)

DBMS( joins)

* **Second round(System design):**

I was asked to explain about my IOT project and draw it’s complete architecture along with hardware and software integration.

A system design requirements were given – Electricity Bill management system – designed a DBMS based system and asked to incorporate any upcoming technologies like ML or AI and make this system more efficient

And few managerial questions were asked about team management, work culture

* **Third round(HR):**

This is basically general questions about you and your profile
And some psychological questions to test your thinking, perception on things

**Tips:**

* Know the company, their work, their product, their reputation in the market, do visit their website, linked in site before you are going for an interview
* Try to make an attempt to any question that they ask to the fullest possible regardless of relevance, correctness and precision, etc…
* Try to be interactive and express your interest whenever possible during the interview and don’t only answer for the interviewer’s question. Being little elaborate on things sometimes is fair.
* Don’t take HR round as it simple as it sounds along with your

cumulative performance in the previous round, HR round also counts

* Be confident and present yourself well regardless of what the background scenario
