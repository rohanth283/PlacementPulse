**Mphasis interview Experience**

**About the Role:**

**Software Engineer ( Intern + Full Time )**

**Selection Process:**

1. **Online Test**
2. **Technical Interview**
3. **HR interview**

**Online Test:**

The online test was conducted on the HirePro platform. It consisted of 55 multiple-choice questions covering Aptitude, English, and Computer Programming, along with one programming (coding) question. Around 96 candidates were shortlisted.

**Round 1 - Technical Interview [40 Mintues] :**

The interview began with my self-introduction, followed by questions about my project. I explained my projects, and the interviewer asked several related questions, which I answered. Later, I was asked two questions on Data Structures, followed by questions on Operating Systems and Database Management Systems (DBMS) AND OOPS. The interviewer also asked about the programming languages I am confident in, specifically Python and Java.

**Round 2- HR Interview: [10 Mintues]**

The HR interview focused on my willingness to relocate and my certifications. I also clarified details regarding my package and joining date. Finally, 60 candidates were shortlisted, and I was one of them.

**My preparation Strategy:**

I practiced problem-solving on LeetCode using the Striver A–Z Sheet. During my initial coding practice, I worked on problems from the ACM problem sheet. I revised core computer science concepts using GeeksforGeeks and InterviewBit, covering DBMS, Operating Systems, OOPS, Computer Networks, and System Design.

In my placement journey, during the initial period, I was not able to clear the online tests. However, after about one and a half months of consistent preparation, I cleared my first online test. That first interview gave me confidence in the placement process. I gained valuable experience and confidence, as I had no prior interview experience except for mock interviews.

Through virtual interviews, I learned the importance of checking camera, microphone, and internet connectivity beforehand to avoid technical issues.

I attended four interviews, and Mphasis was the fifth company where I attended an interview. I performed well, but I was not selected for the next round. During that time, I experienced a lot of stress and self-doubt. However, I learned an important lesson: trust the process. Do not lose hope. Prepare consistently and never give up. Patience is key in the placement journey—never lose hope and trust the process.
