| CANDIDATE | PROFILE:  |     |     |     |
The most important thing is resume. A lot of templates are available on websites with the help
of which you can develop one. I used Canva to build my resume. It can be divided into the
following sections – personal details, educational qualification, projects (a short description of
each – what it is and technologies used), skills, co-curricular activities and
certifications/achievements (if any). You can highlight your best projects if you have worked on
many. It is advisable to fit your resume to one page and add your photo too. Also, you should be
completely aware of what you add in your resume because interviewers will ask a lot of
questions based on it. So do not add anything that you don’t know.
