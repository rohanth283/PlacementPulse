      ENGINEER TRAINEE IN AMERICAN EXPRESS

About the Company:

The American Express Company, or Amex, is an American bank holding company and multinational
financial services corporation specializing in payment cards. They leverage advanced data analytics,
cloud infrastructure, secure APIs, machine learning, and a hybrid cloud environment to provide a
seamless, secure, and personalized experience for users. The company’s technological investments
support not only transaction processing but also fraud prevention, regulatory compliance, and
customer engagement, making it a leader in the digital finance space.

About the role:

The Engineer Trainee role at American Express involves developing scalable, mission-critical
applications on cloud platforms using languages such as Java, Python, and JavaScript. The work spans
various tech domains, including real-time transaction processing, big data, AI/ML, and omnichannel
solutions for mobile and social integrations. The role includes collaborating with skilled engineers on
design, coding, and Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) tasks. It also fosters a culture of
innovation, continuous learning, and experimentation with cutting-edge technologies.

Overview of the process:

Online coding test:

The coding test was conducted on the Codility platform. It included three coding questions, focused
on String manipulation, Greedy approach and Graph. Only three test cases were visible, while the
rest were hidden. Scores were based on the number of hidden test cases that passed, so it was
essential to handle all edge cases correctly for successful completion.
