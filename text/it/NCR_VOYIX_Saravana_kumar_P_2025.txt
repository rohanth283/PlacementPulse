**Preparation Strategy & Advice:**

1. **Start Early:** Although I started preparing towards the end of my third year, I recommend starting earlier to have more time for practice.
2. **Focus on Fundamentals:** It is crucial to have a strong understanding of DBMS, OOPS, and DSA concepts. I found Striver Sheet useful for practicing DSA.
3. **Problem-Solving Approach:**
   * Start with a brute-force solution.
   * Progress to more optimized approaches with explanations on time and space complexity.
4. **Theory Topics:** Use resources like InterviewBit and GeeksforGeeks for theory preparation on topics like OOPS, DBMS, OS, and Cloud technologies.
5. **Soft Skills:** Communication is key. Practice explaining your problem-solving process and your experiences clearly.
6. **Behavioral Questions:** For behavioral questions, I used examples from my projects, internships, and extracurricular activities to demonstrate my skills and experience.
7. **Learn from Seniors:** Speaking with seniors who’ve gone through the interview process gave me valuable insights into the company’s recruitment patterns.
8. **Stay Calm:** It is important to remain calm and methodical throughout the interview process. Keeping a clear head helps in approaching problems effectively.

Overall, my interview experience at NCR Voyix was highly engaging and tested my technical, problem-solving, and communication skills thoroughly.
