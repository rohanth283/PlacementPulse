**TRICKS THAT I FOLLOWED** :

* First of all, Have patience, prepare consistently and hold out hope.
* I used ACM materials and Striver’s lecture helped me a lot and indiabix website for aptitude and few other materials.
* But meanwhile, try to build your resume which is equally important when you get selected for an interview.
* At least be thorough in the basics and in Arrays, Strings, hash maps and better know the rest of DSA concepts if not how to code.
* Also prepare yourself to tackle any sort of questions from your any nook and corner of your resume
* Mostly in interviews, they will try to check your understanding about the concepts.
* While going for an interview, make sure you visit their website and have a decent knowledge about that company and get some suggestions from someone who already gone for the same interview the previous year or someone who worked there.
