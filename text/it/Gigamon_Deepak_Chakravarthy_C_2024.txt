My interview experience with Gigamon

Hi, I’m Deepak Chakravarthy C. I have completed my B.Tech in IT and have now joined
Gigamon as a Software Quality Assistant. I am writing to document and share my
experiences during the interview process at Gigamon, which took place on 25th April 2024.
appreciate the opportunity to have been considered for this role, and I want to provide a
comprehensive overview of my interview experiences.

1.  Initial Application and Screening:

I initially applied on April 5th through CUIC. Shortly after, on April 8th, I received
communication from Gigamon inviting me to participate in an online test. The test was based
on aptitude reasoning and network knowledge, as Gigamon is a network-based company.

2.  First Round Interview (Technical):

I was called to participate in the interview process on April 25th, 2023. My first-round
interview tested my basics in programming and networking. I was asked to explain my past
projects, and there were also some questions on data structures.

3.  Second Round Interview (Techno-manegerial):

In the second round of the interview, I was once again tested on my knowledge of
networking and asked about my past projects, including my final-year project. The
interviewer presented some situational scenarios and asked how I would respond. I believe I
responded well.

4.  Final Round Interview (HR round):

In the HR round, I was asked about my personal details, past interview experiences, and how
I would handle certain workplace situations. We also discussed my career goals, both short-
term and long-term.
