1) Buy and sell stocks

Given an array of stock prices for any number of days. Buy stock

2) Select a seat in a theater

Given an array of a row in a theater consisting of 0’s and 1’s, 0
denoting empty seats and 1 denoting filled seat. Select an empty
seat in the array, where the nearest filled seat in the left and right
direction is at the maximum distance.
