About the Role:

As a Summer Intern at Fidelity Investments, the role involves collaborating with experienced
professionals on real-time projects in areas like software development, cybersecurity, and data
analysis. The internship offers exposure to various tools, technologies, and processes used in the
financial services sector. Interns are expected to contribute actively to ongoing projects while
enhancing their technical and problem-solving skills, gaining insights into the dynamic intersection of
technology and finance.

Preparation Strategy:

For preparation, I focused on strengthening my fundamentals in data structures and algorithms
(DSA), object-oriented programming (OOP) in C++, and understanding version control systems like
Git. I reviewed my past projects and internships to clearly explain their technical aspects and
challenges. For HR preparation, I prepared answers to common questions about motivation,
preferred career field, and personal interests. I also practiced mock interviews and used online
coding platforms like LeetCode for DSA practice.

Placement Drive Experience:

The placement drive at Fidelity involved an initial online test that covered technical concepts,
aptitude, and coding. The technical interview focused on my projects, basic C++ concepts, data
structures, and algorithms for app development. The HR interview explored my extracurricular
activities, motivations, and career preferences, along with a discussion on personal interests and my
recent reading.

Tips and Tricks:

To succeed, focus on mastering core subjects like data structures, algorithms, and object-oriented
programming. Be clear about the technical details of your projects and internships, and practice
explaining your contributions. Communication is key, so prepare for group discussions and HR
interviews by working on your speaking skills. Regular coding practice on platforms like HackerRank
or LeetCode can help with the technical rounds. Most importantly, stay calm and confident during
the interview process.
