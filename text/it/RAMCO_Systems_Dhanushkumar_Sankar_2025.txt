Previous Interview Experience
In my previous interview at Everstage, DSA helped me reach the last round.
However, I was rejected due to weak theoretical knowledge. This taught me the
importance of balancing DSA preparation with theory subjects. Don't ignore theory;
study it alongside DSA.

Preparation Strategy

DSA
DSA highlights your ability to solve problems. Focus on Striver’s SDE Sheet and
Leetcode Interview 150 for targeted preparation.

Resources:

- Striver's playlists on YouTube.

- Striver’s resources provide everything needed to excel in DSA.

Revise these playlists before interviews to showcase your skills effectively.

Projects
Know your project thoroughly. Common questions include:

1. Why did you choose this tech stack/database? Why not another?
2. What was your role?
3. Explain the architecture and flow of your project.
4. Challenges faced during implementation.

Avoid adding fake projects to your resume. Learn basic GitHub commands to
demonstrate project management knowledge.

SQL
Practice basic SQL Questions and try to do SQL 50 from LeetCode

Resume
Keep your resume concise, listing only the skills you're confident about. Companies
don’t expect you to know their tech stack but want to assess how well you can apply
what you know.

Theory Subjects
Focus on key topics from InterviewBit and GeeksforGeeks (GFG):
- OOPs: OOPs Sheet, GFG.
- DBMS: InterviewBit, GFG.
- OS: GFG.
- CN: GFG.
- Git: W3Schools.

Understand these concepts thoroughly instead of memorizing everything.
