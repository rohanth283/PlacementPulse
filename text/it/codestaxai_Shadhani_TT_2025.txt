**Overview of the Process:**

The overall process consisted of

* An Online coding contest in Hackerrank
* Interview Round 1 (Technical) – in Department
* Interview Round 2 (Technical) – Online mode

**Online coding contest in Hackerrank:**

The coding contest was conducted in Hackerrank. It consisted of 4 coding questions.

1. Count Pairs
2. Matching Braces
3. Min Processing Time
4. Get Maximum Power

18 students were shortlisted for (Round 1) interview process.

**Interview Round 1 (Technical) – Offline mode:**

This round went for 50 to 55 minutes.

* They started by asking me to introduce myself.
* They asked about a problem I face in my daily life and how I solved it.
* They inquired about a challenge I encountered during my project and the steps I took to resolve it.
* They posed some questions related to my project.
* They asked, “What is a subnet?” and related it to my project.
* They gave a question about subsets and expected me to solve it by drawing a tree.
* They asked me to write and explain the logic for Merge Sort.
* They requested me to write a program or pseudocode for Merge Sort.
* Any questions for him?

**Interview Round 2 (Technical) – Online mode:**

This round went for 30 minutes.

* They began by asking me to introduce myself.
* I was asked to explain all my projects.
* The interviewer highlighted one specific project, asked me to explain it in detail, and followed up with some related questions.
* I was tasked to code a "Two Sum" program using an online compiler.
