Mode :-  Offline At CUIC

Duration :- 40 mins
Number of Shortlists:- 20

background,  and  how  I  balanced  my  academics  with  extracurricular

management. I was also asked about my short-term and long-term goals.

logical thinking.

•  Basic  questions  on  Object-Oriented  Programming  (OOP)

concepts.

•  Questions based on my resume regarding my experience with AWS

and Google Cloud, including where and how I used them.

HR discussions. It began with general questions such as introducing

curricular experiences.
