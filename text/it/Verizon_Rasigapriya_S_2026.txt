About the company

Verizon Communications is a leading telecommunications provider and one of the world’s foremost companies in
technology, communications, and network solutions. Consistently ranked among the Fortune 500, the company was
established in 2000 and serves over 100 million wireless customers. It delivers advanced broadband, fiber-optic, and
5G connectivity to businesses and consumers across the United States and internationally. Verizon operates the largest
4G LTE network in the U.S. The company is headquartered in New York City and maintains a significant presence in
India, which offices in Chennai, Bangalore, and Hyderabad.

About the Role

The position offered was Engineer I (Software Development). During the discussion, the interviewer and HR
explained that the role involves working with a range of technologies and requires flexibility to contribute across
multiple domains.

Interview Process

ROUND 1: Online Assessment

The first round consisted of multiple components. The initial section required candidates to write a composition of
approximately 190-250 words. The topic assigned was “Is it important for celebrities to be good citizens to help and
guide society through their thoughts and actions?”. This section primarily evaluated general verbal and writing skills
rather than technical knowledge.

The second component was a coding round that included two programming questions of easy to medium difficulty.
This was followed by a verbal and communication assessment that test vocabulary and grammar proficiency.
Additionally, the round included psychometric questions designed to evaluate personality traits, behavioural
tendencies, and overall suitability for the role.

ROUND 2: Group Discussion

This round assessed candidates’ communication skills, clarity of thought, and ability to present their opinions clearly.
Participants were expected to express their views confidently while maintaining professionalism. It was important not
to be overly forceful, to contribute at appropriate moments, and to ensure that the points presented were meaningful
and impactful.

My panel consisted of 10 members, and the discussion was moderated by a senior HR representative. The moderator
instructed us to initiate the discussion with a topic provided by them under the domain of AI. I contributed 4 points
during the session, focusing on presenting concise and clear points. At the end of the round, 7 candidates were selected
to proceed to the next stage.

A key recommendation for this round is to regularly follow current technology news updated on recent developments,
as this helped me in presenting informed and relevant viewpoints during the discussion.

ROUND 3: AI Prompting Round

The third round was an AI prompting round. Candidates were provided with a set of problem statements and were
required to design effective prompts that could generate accurate and relevant results. The objective of this round was
to evaluate the ability to frame clear and efficient prompts for optimal output.

This was not an elimination round. However, the responses submitted were cumulatively evaluated and considered
during the technical interview.

ROUND 4: Technical Interview

The technical interview was conducted in a comfortable and interactive manner, with a friendly approach throughout
the session. He began by reviewing my resume and asked questions about my previous internship experiences, and the
project currently going through.

The discussion primarily focused on the project I completed during my internship. I was asked questions related to
security concepts, authentication mechanisms, and UI optimization. In addition, DSA problems were discussed, and I
was asked to write an optimal solution. I initially presented a brute-force approach and subsequently refined it into an
optimal solution based on the interviewer’s follow-up questions.

Towards the end, I was given the opportunity to ask questions. I inquired about the interviewer’s experience within the
company, and the areas within the company where AI is applied and its role. The interviewer responded clearly and
provided detailed insights.

ROUND 5: HR Interview

In the HR interview, I was asked to provide an overview of my resume and respond to general questions regarding my
location preference and the knowledge I carried about the company.

During this round, I took the opportunity to ask questions about the company’s work culture and growth opportunities
and about the role I will be working on. Asking relevant questions is encouraged as it shows your interest and
enthusiasm you possess for the role.

A total of 32 candidates were selected by the company, and the confirmation mails were sent on the same day.

TIPS & TRICKS

•  Maintain confidence and communicate your ideas clearly and effectively.
•  Pay equal attention to verbal and communication skills, along with technical preparation.
•  Be thorough with your resume and present your experiences authentically. Do not overload your resume. Be

precise.

•  Strength your understanding of core technical subjects, particularly DSA and DBMS.
