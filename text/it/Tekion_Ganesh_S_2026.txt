About Tekion
Tekion Corp is a cloud technology company founded in 2016 by former Tesla CIO Jay Vijayan.
ThecompanyisheadquarteredinPleasanton, California, withAsia-Pacificoperationsincluding
a major technology center in Chennai, India.
The company’s flagship platform, Automotive Retail Cloud (ARC), is an end-to-end,
cloud-native solution designed for automotive dealerships. It integrates multiple modules such
as Customer Relationship Management (CRM), Dealer Management System (DMS), digital
retail, service management, parts, analytics, and AI-powered insights.
In 2022, Tekion also introduced the Automotive Enterprise Cloud (AEC), a unified
digital ecosystem that enables vehicle purchases directly from OEM brand websites through
dealerships. It offers configurator tools, e-signature options, online payments, and advanced
analytics—bridging the gap between manufacturers and dealerships.

My Interview Process and Experience
The Tekion recruitment process I went through was structured across five main stages. Each
round had a distinct purpose, focusing on different skill sets.
Round 1: Online Assessment
This was the initial shortlisting stage I took online. It included:
• 8 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) covering DBMS, Operating Systems, and Data Struc-
tures & Algorithms (DSA).
• 1 medium-level coding question (example: split-array pattern problem).
• 1 SQL query-based problem to test database knowledge.
From this round, approximately 50 candidates including myself were shortlisted for the next
stage.
—
Round 2: Offline Coding Round
The second round I attended took place in an offline setting with two categories of tasks:
• Frontend Task: I had to build a form interface supporting multiple input types, a preview
option, and JSON export functionality. My implementation was around 90% complete.
• Backend Tasks: I solved algorithmic problems such as the “Buy and Sell Stock” optimiza-
tion and a visitor log analysis problem.
About 20 candidates including myself advanced to the next stage.
