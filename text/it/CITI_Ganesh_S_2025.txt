4.3 HR Interview
This round was short for most candidates due to time constraints, but I extended mine by
preparing well:
• The interviewer asked if there was anything I had not mentioned in my resume. I shared
real-world problem-solving projects, which were not part of the coursework projects listed
on my resume.
• I discussed my experience with a project aimed at helping the homeless, pitched during
a 7-day camp organized by Thozhan. The interviewer appreciated my initiative and
motivation.
• We also had a conversation about hobbies and work-life balance. The interviewer asked
what I would be doing if there was no engineering domain, and I answered ”service.”

| and queues | to stay in | a safe zone. |     |
