Mini Gnana Sekaran- Information Technology –6 months intern at Logitech

About the Company: Logitech is a globally recognized technology company specializing in
designing and manufacturing computer peripherals and software. The company is known for its
innovative approach to product development, delivering high-quality solutions in areas such as
gaming, video collaboration, and AI-driven advancements. With a strong focus on user
experience and technological innovation, Logitech continuously evolves to meet the needs of
both consumers and businesses.

About the Role:

Logitech offered two job roles for this internship:

1.  Frontend Development
2.  Generative AI

Overview of the Process:

The selection process included three rounds:

1.  Written Test (Technical Aptitude + Domain-Specific)
2.  Technical Interview (DSA, OOP, OS, Resume-Based)
3.  HR Interview (Behavioral, Leadership, and Project Discussion)

Interview Process

Round 1: Written Test

●  The written test consisted of two sections:

○  C++ Programming Questions (Mandatory for all candidates)
○  Specialized Test: Candidates could choose between a JavaScript test (for the

Frontend role) or a Generative AI test.

●  The test evaluated problem-solving skills, coding proficiency, and understanding of

role-specific concepts.

Round 2: Technical Interview

●  Data Structures and Algorithms: I was tested on various DSA topics, requiring efficient

implementations in C++.

●  Object-Oriented Programming: Questions focused on core OOP principles, including

inheritance, polymorphism, and design patterns.

●  Operating Systems: The interview covered key OS concepts such as memory

management, process scheduling, and concurrency.

●  Resume-Based Questions: The interviewer asked in-depth questions about my

projects, the tech stack used, and my problem-solving approach.

●  Leadership & Teamwork: I was asked about my leadership experiences, team projects,

and how I handled challenges.

Round 3: HR Interview

●  Resume Discussion: The HR round focused on my previous experiences, including

internships, projects, and extracurricular leadership roles.

●  Project Discussion: I explained the project I would be working on at Logitech, detailing

the technologies involved and expected contributions.

●  Behavioral Questions: Questions included how I handle conflicts, work in a team, and

adapt to new technologies.

Preparation Strategy

●  DSA & Problem Solving: Practiced coding problems, focusing on commonly asked

DSA topics.

●  OOP & OS Concepts: Revised core principles, ensuring a strong understanding of

system design and OS fundamentals.

●  Domain-Specific Knowledge: Prepared for the JavaScript and GenAI test by revisiting

frameworks, tools, and recent advancements in AI.

●  Company Research: Studied Logitech’s latest innovations, products, and company

culture to align my answers with their goals.

Tips & Tricks

●  Choose the Right Track: Pick a test paper (JavaScript or GenAI) based on your

strengths and interests.

●  Master the Basics: Focus on strong foundational knowledge in DSA, OOP, and OS.
●  Showcase Leadership & Problem-Solving: Be ready to explain how you led teams,

tackled challenges, and optimized solutions.

●  Ask Meaningful Questions: Engage with the interviewer by asking about the work

environment, projects, and growth opportunities.
