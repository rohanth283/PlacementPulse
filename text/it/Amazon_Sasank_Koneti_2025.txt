| Must Do | in Coding interview: |           |     |     |
1)Think out Loud: Articulate your thought process and reasoning during problem-solving to keep

2)Understand the Crux of the problem: Before jumping into the solution, make sure you fully
grasp the problem by asking any needed clarifying questions.Make observations from the
problem
3)Approach: Start with a Brute-Force Solution. Discuss the brute-force approach initially to show
you can identify a basic starting point. Then, work on optimizing the solution if possible.
| General | Preparation | Tips: |     |     |
| ------- | ----------- | ----- | --- | --- |
When practicing coding questions, start with daily problems on LeetCode and gradually
transition to competitive programming sites like CodeForces. Instead of relying solely on
YouTube tutorials, focus on learning from problem editorials. Use SDE sheets primarily for
revision rather than as your primary resource. Understand how much time to allocate to a
specific problem, and make sure to consistently participate in contests to improve your rating
and time management skills. Above all, maintain consistency in your practice.
