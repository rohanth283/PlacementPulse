Intern Drive Experience – Accenture
Manish M (IT Purple)

About the company:

Accenture is a global professional services company offering services in strategy, consulting,
digital, technology, and operations. It helps businesses improve performance and navigate
change through technology and industry expertise. Accenture has a notable presence in India
and across the globe, working in areas such as digital transformation, cloud, and AI to assist
businesses in their growth.

Role: Advanced Application Engineering Intern

Round 1 – Cognitive and Technical Assessment:

The test consisted of 90 questions to be completed in 90 minutes. It was divided into six
sections covering topics like verbal, aptitude, reasoning, pseudocode outputs, MS Office, and
basics of networking and cloud.

Those who cleared the test proceeded to the coding assessment, which took place immediately
afterwards.

Round 2 – Coding Assessment:

There were two coding questions, and 45 minutes were provided. Both the questions were
fairly straightforward and involved arrays and sorting for me. One thing to note is that you had
to handle both the input and output yourself, rather than just filling in a function as in typical
tests. Those who are unfamiliar with this can find similar questions on platforms like CodeChef
and Codeforces.

68 students were shortlisted after this round.

Round 3 – Communication Assessment:

The test took about half an hour and was AI-based. It assessed reading, listening, and speaking
skills through tasks such as reading sentences aloud, repeating phrases, and retelling a narrated
story.

This was not an elimination round.

Round 4 – Virtual Interview:

The interview was held online on Accenture’s platform and was 25-30 minutes long. My
interview was not too technical, focusing instead on general and behavioural questions.
The interviewer concentrated on my projects, discussing the experiences and skills gained
while working on them. Apart from that, there were questions about academics, as well as
some HR-related ones.

Five students were shortlisted for the internship.

Preparation Tips:

  Keep Leetcoding and keep strengthening your DSA.
  Build a strong resume, highlighting projects, certifications, achievements, and

anything else that adds value.

  Focus on projects and explore trending areas like AI and machine learning.
  For coding rounds, practice a wide range of problems and participate in contests

across various platforms.

  For interviews, first and foremost, ensure you have a strong resume with solid
projects. Be thorough with each of your projects and prepare to answer any
questions about them. Be well-versed in OS, DBMS, OOP, SQL. and have strong
communication skills. Prepare for frequently asked questions and go through past
interview questions and experiences.
