My placement experience consisted of three rounds :

This round was conducted on HackerRank. It consisted of four coding questions: two easy problems and two
hard problems.

Round 2: Technical Interview

This round was conducted on Zoom. It included a discussion about my projects, followed by technical

Round 3: Technical Interview

This round was conducted on Zoom. It started with a discussion about my resume, followed by questions on

PREPARATION STRATEGY:

My approach to preparation involved practicing coding on various platforms like LeetCode, HackerRank, and
GeeksforGeeks. I began with basic and easy questions to get comfortable with the coding language and then
progressed to more challenging medium and hard problems It’s essential to learn different algorithms, such as
two pointers, sliding window, greedy methods, and dynamic programming, as these techniques are crucial for
solving various problems. I found that dynamic programming and subsequence problems are often featured in
tests. Understanding time and space complexity is also important, as these questions come up in both initial
assessments and interviews. For learning OOPs concepts, I used resources from the internet(geeksforgeeks
and javatpoint).
