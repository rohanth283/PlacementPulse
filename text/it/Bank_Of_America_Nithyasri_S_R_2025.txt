                   Bank of America(BoA) is a multinational financial institution offering wide
range of banking and financial services to individuals, businesses, and corporations. It
is renowned for its consumer banking, wealth management through investment
banking, and global markets operations. The bank is recognized for its innovation in

About the role

The freshers from our college are initially recruited for the role of GET (Graduate
Engineering Trainee) but the campus role at BoA offers opportunities across diverse
areas.
1. Development: Building advanced FinTech solutions for banking, trading, and risk
using technologies like AI, ML, and cloud, focusing on areas like algorithmic trading,
anti-money laundering, and digital banking.

2. Testing: Ensuring product quality through functional and technical testing, risk
assessment, and performance optimization using tools like Selenium and JIRA.

3. Risk Management: Entry-level analysis in cloud governance, cybersecurity, and
operational risk to enhance BoA's control environment.

4. Infrastructure Operations: Software development to meet both functional and
non-functional business needs, focusing on DevOps, testing, and automated release.

5. Production Support: Event management, incident handling, and maintaining high
availability in network and infrastructure operations.

6. Cybersecurity: Safeguarding systems against cyber threats with technologies like
SIEM,Splunk, and identity management tools.

7. Identity & Access Management (IAM): Controlling access and managing
compliance across various tech environments, ensuring security and governance.

8. Core Security: Monitoring for security incidents, vulnerability assessments, and
ethical hacking to protect BoA’s digital assets.
