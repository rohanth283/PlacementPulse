**Round 2: Online Assessment (Coding-@CUIC)**

* The round consisted of **6 questions (3 Python + 3 SQL)**.
* There were **no visible test cases**, and it appeared to be **AI-based code evaluation**.
* I was comparatively stronger in **SQL than Python**, so I chose to **solve the SQL questions first**.
* In Python, one question was **Longest Substring Without Repeating Characters** .
* I could not clearly remember the other two Python questions.

* The final SQL question was **Employees earning more than their managers**, solved using a **SELF JOIN**.
* The round mainly tested **Python problem-solving skills and core SQL concepts, especially window functions**.
* Due to the limited duration of the round, I could **finish 4 out of the 6 problems**.
* Around **15 candidates were shortlisted** for the next technical round.

[45 mins]
