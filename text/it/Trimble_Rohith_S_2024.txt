**Preparation Strategy**

1.Communication Skills: Develop strong communication skills to build confidence and clarity, which is crucial for interactions with interviewers.

2. Coding Practice: Focus on coding problems, especially on platforms like LeetCode, targeting key areas like arrays and strings, as these concepts are frequently tested.

3. Approach to Problem-Solving: Even if you are familiar with a problem, act as if you are encountering it for the first time. Provide the best solution without dragging out the explanation.

4. Concept Clarity: Be precise with the concepts and terminology you use. Avoid mentioning topics you are unsure of, as interviewers often dive deeper based on your responses.

**Placement Drive Experience**

- Online Test: Focus on passing test cases efficiently, considering both accuracy and time taken, to clear this round successfully.

- Technical Interview: Maintain clarity on concepts, avoid mentioning uncertain topics, and handle each question with confidence. If stuck, move on to the next question to manage time effectively.

- HR Interview: Beyond technical skills, emotional intelligence (EQ) is evaluated. Showcase leadership qualities and handle situational questions tactfully.

**Tips and Tricks**

- If you find yourself unclear or stuck during an answer, smoothly shift the conversation toward your areas of strength.
