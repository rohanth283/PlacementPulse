About the Company

CodeStax.Ai is a cutting-edge company revolutionizing digital transformation with AI-powered, no-
code platforms. Focused on democratizing technology, CodeStax.Ai empowers businesses of all sizes
to innovate and adapt rapidly to the digital landscape. The company values transparency,

About the Role

Role: Software Development Engineer (SDE)
As an SDE at CodeStax.Ai, you will work on building and optimizing AI-powered, no-code platforms,
contributing to innovative solutions that transform the way businesses operate. The role involves
problem-solving, coding, and leveraging data structures and algorithms to create scalable solutions.

Preparation Strategy

•  Programming Fundamentals: Strengthen problem-solving skills by practicing on platforms

like LeetCode, HackerRank, and GeeksforGeeks.

•  Data Structures & Algorithms: Focus on arrays, strings, sorting, priority queues, graphs, and

dynamic programming.

•  Resume Projects: Be ready to discuss your projects in depth, especially technologies like

Spring Boot, APIs, and database interactions.

•  Puzzles and Logical Thinking: Prepare for puzzles, such as the 100 doors problem.

•  Mock Interviews: Practice technical and behavioral questions to improve confidence.
