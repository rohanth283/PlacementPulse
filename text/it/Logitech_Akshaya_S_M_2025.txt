LOGITECH (6 MONTH INTERNSHIP)

Process:

•  The entire process was held at logitech office(Olympia tech

park,Guindy).

•  There were 4 rounds totally.

MCQ (round-1):

•  There were two types of MCQ (C++ and Frontend).
•  Those who scored above 30 out of 50 were shortlisted next round.

Technical interview-1(Frontend):

•  This round was entirely based on frontend projects.
•  Since I have deployed a website, most of the questions were

based on it.

•  Then they asked to write some react and javascript codes for

some functionalities of the website.

Technical interview-2(C++):

•  In this round DSA was primarily focussed.
•  Maximum subarray(Kadane’s algorithm) was asked.
•  Then they asked to explain the projects in my resume.
•  Pascal’s triangle along with time complexity was also asked.

HR interview

•  Tell me about yourself.
•  Explain non technical skills.
•  Some HR questions like where do you see yourself in 5 years.
