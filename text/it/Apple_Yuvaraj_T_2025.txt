  Coding Question: (it is a leetcode question)

Imagine you're playing a game where you need to cross a sequence of stones to reach the final stone at
the end, and reaching it will save your life. Each stone has a number on it that indicates the maximum
number of steps you can jump forward from that stone. Starting from the first stone, your objective is to
determine whether you can make it to the last stone. Return true if it's possible to reach the last stone,
otherwise return false.

Input:[2, 3, 1, 1, 4]

Output: 1

I/O Explanation:

You can jump 1 step from the first stone to the second stone, and then take 3 steps to jump directly to
the last stone.
