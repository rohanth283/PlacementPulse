**Overview of the Process:**

The overall process consisted of

* Online Interview Round 1(HR)-in virtual
* Online Interview Round 2(Technical)-in Virtual

**Interview Round 1 (Based on Resume):**

It went for nearly 10 minutes. The HR asked me to introduce myself including projects, branch, my hobbies etc…

* He asked about my family background, my native,open to relocation.
* Why do you want to work here when there are so many IT companies?
* What do you know about the company?
* Where do you urself 5 years from now?
* Any doubts to ask for him.

He seemed quite satisfied with my communication and resume.

**Interview Round 2 (Technical):**

It went for 30 minutes through virtual mode. He asked to give a brief introduction about myself. After that, he asked a few technical questions.

1. How do you reverse a doubly linked list?
2. What’s the difference between a singly linked list and a doubly linked list?
3. How do you perform binary search on a sorted array?
4. What is the difference between BFS and DFS?
5. How do you check if a string is a palindrome?
6. What is deadlock? What are the four necessary conditions for a deadlock?
7. What is the difference between a process and a thread?
8. Can you explain the OSI model and its layers?
9. What are some common application layer protocols and their uses?
10. What is the difference between TCP and UDP?

He seemed quite impressed with my communication skills. When I started answering, he was paying close attention to see if I could respond well to the questions. After I answered a single question, he quickly moved on to the next one, which made the pace feel fast.
