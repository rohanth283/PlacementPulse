**Technical Round 1 :**

* I was provided with a link to join the interview. Once the interviewer joined, he introduced himself and asked me to do the same. He then started reviewing my resume, inquiring about my familiarity with the programming languages I had listed.
* I was asked to write a code for reversing a string using Notepad while sharing my screen. I presented several approaches, including slicing, using a stack, and a loop-based method.
* Since I used Python, he tested my understanding of the language by asking basic questions, such as the difference between a list and a tuple.
* The conversation then shifted to my internship during my 4th-semester vacation, where I thoroughly explained the project, I worked on.
* He also asked a few questions related to the technologies I used during that internship.

Finally, he asked if I had any questions for him. The interview lasted for 45 minutes.

**Interview Round 2 (Technical):**
