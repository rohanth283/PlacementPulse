Online coding contest in SHL platform:

The coding contest was conducted in SHL platform. All the instructions given by

the platform should be followed strictly like turning ON camera, microphones. The test is
of 4 sections namely 2 aptitude sections and 2 coding sections.

The technical rules are you can’t move to the next question without answering

the current question. And once you have moved from a question, you can’t move to that
question. No negative markings.

The aptitude section:

•  English aptitude.
•  Business Analytics.

The 3rd section consisted of 2 coding questions. (40 mis)

1.  Find the count of non-repeating alphabets in the given string.

2.  You have two arrays with repeating positive integers. The count of the non-

common numbers from the two arrays must be returned.

The 4th section consisted of only one question. (10 mins)

1.  Graph Problem – The Famous Konigsberg Bridge Problem is described in detail
and our task is to find the given graph ends up like the given bridge or can it be
solvable. Then return true if solvable or false if unsolvable.

22 students were shortlisted for interview process of Intern Analyst.

TIPS for Aptitude Sections:

•  Have a basic English Knowledge. Connect the question with each of the 4 given
options. If you don’t know the answer then don’t get stressed, stay calm and
answer according to your conscious, believe on your 1st choice.

•  Logical Thinking is the key along with some maths. For business analytics kind of
questions, read the entire question because the answer will be just hidden in.

•  Practice speed math, just a transferable skill. Trust me it helps a lot.
