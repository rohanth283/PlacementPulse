Online Assessment:

The online assessment was conducted via a Microsoft Teams meeting with a
duration of 1 hour and 30 minutes. During the session, four questions were
displayed, primarily focusing on flowcharts and instruction-based problems.
Each question included several sub-questions. Candidates wrote their answers in
a notebook and submitted them as a PDF document.

Online Aptitude Test:

The online aptitude test included questions on Object-Oriented Programming
(OOPs), Database Management Systems (DBMS), Operating Systems (OS),
Networking, and Node.js. The test had a duration of one hour, with
approximately 60 questions.

Interview Round 1(Technical):

The technical interview lasted for approximately 30 minutes and was primarily
resume-based, with questions focused on topics mentioned in the resume.

Interview Questions:

•  Tell me about yourself.

•  Do you know JavaScript? If so, which version or framework of

JavaScript do you know?

•  What is ReactJS?

•  What are React Hooks?

•  Explain useState and useEffect in React.

•  What is PySide6?

•  What is polymorphism?

•  What are primary keys and foreign keys in databases?

•  What is the most recent project you are working on?

•  Do you have any questions?

Data Structures & Algorithms (DSA) Questions:

•  Reverse a string using recursion.

•  Reverse a string using a queue.

•  Find the duplicate letters in a string.

The interview was focused on concepts and projects listed in the resume,
ensuring a discussion aligned with the candidate’s experience and expertise.

Interview Round 2(Managerial):

The 30-minute managerial interview focused on personal background, technical
interests, career aspirations, and problem-solving abilities.

Interview Questions:

•  Tell me about your family and technical interests.

•  What is your dream career?

•  What responsibilities have you handled in both technical and non-

technical roles?

•  What is your professional dream?

•  What makes you happy in the IT field?

•  What do you consider your biggest achievement in the IT field?

•  Have you ever noticed any bugs in social media platforms or online
applications? If so, what was the issue, and how would you fix it?

•  Do you have any startup ideas?

•  What challenges have you faced in your projects, and how did you

overcome them?

The discussion emphasized problem-solving skills, innovative thinking, and
career aspirations in the IT domain.

HR Round:

The HR interview was conducted online and lasted for 10 minutes, scheduled
four days after the managerial interview.

Interview Questions & Experience:

•  How was your previous interview?

•  What other company interviews have you attended before?

•  Why do you think you were not selected in those interviews?

After answering these questions, the interviewer remained silent for 3 to 5
minutes, asking the candidate to wait. This happened to all three candidates.

The interview concluded with a discussion about salary.
