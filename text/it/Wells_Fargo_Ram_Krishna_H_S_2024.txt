About the Company:

Wells Fargo is a major American financial services company headquartered in
San Francisco, California, with significant operations across the United States.
Founded in 1852, it offers a wide range of services including banking,
mortgage, investments, and consumer and commercial finance. The company is
known for its extensive branch network and ATMs, making it one of the largest
banks in the U.S. by assets.

About the Role:

CCIBT – Commercial And Corporate Investment Banking Technology

Commercial and Corporate Investment Banking Technology (CCIBT) refers to
the specialized technological infrastructure and solutions that support the
operations of commercial and corporate investment banking services. This
sector involves developing, maintaining, and enhancing software platforms,
trading systems, risk management tools, and data analytics solutions that enable
banks to manage complex financial transactions, investment portfolios, and
corporate client relationships. CCIBT plays a critical role in ensuring the
efficiency, security, and compliance of banking operations, especially in areas
like automated trading, real-time data processing, and regulatory reporting. As
the financial industry increasingly adopts digital transformation, CCIBT is at the
forefront, driving innovation and enabling banks to remain competitive in a
rapidly evolving market.
