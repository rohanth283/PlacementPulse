Citi Corp recruitment happened for our campus in August 2020. 
Online Test: 
               
             The test was for 2 hours. It had 4 sections. 
 
 Logical Reasoning (14 MCQ’s) 
 Quantitative Aptitude and English (16 MCQ’s) 
 Technical (25 MCQ’s) 
 2 Coding questions. 
 
Technical Interview: 
 
It happened on the aspiring minds platforms  due to COVID situations. 
There were 2 panelists. They asked me to introduce myself. Then they 
started asking questions about the skills I mentioned in my resume and intro. 
 
HR Interview: 
                This will be mostly a formality. The panelist asked me only 3 to 4 
questions regarding my interests and personality. There is a possibility of 
asking questions from your projects. It hardly lasted for 15 minutes.
