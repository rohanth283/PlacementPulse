Question 1: 
Get Maximum Memory Difference (Greedy) 
 You are given an array of integers and an integer k, you have to select k elements from the array so 
that the sum of absolute difference between each pairs in the k selected elements is the maximum. You 
must return the maximum sum. 
 
Example:  
Input: array = [2,3,1,2] k = 3 
Output: 4 
Explanation: 
[2,1,2] = | 2 – 1 | + | 1 – 2 | + | 2 – 2| = 2 
[2,3,1] = | 2 – 3 | + | 2 – 1 | + | 3 – 1 | = 4 
[2,3,2] = | 2 – 2 | + | 2 – 3 | + | 3 – 2 | = 2 
[3,1,2] = | 3 – 1 | + | 1 – 2 | + | 3 – 2 | = 4 
The answer is 4.
