1. Online test 
 
The online test consisted of 3 coding questions and was conducted on 
codility platform. 
Questions: 
i) Given an array of integers. Find a subarray that starts and ends with the same 
number and has the maximum possible sum. Return that maximum sum. 
 
ii) given a row of houses, each containing bags of different types of trash: 
Metal (M), Plastic (P), and Glass (G). Distances between consecutive houses 
are given in array D, and the trash types at each house are given in array T. 
Each type of trash has a dedicated truck that: 
• Starts at the beginning, 
• Travels to the farthest house containing its trash type, 
• Collects bags (1 minute per bag), 
• Returns to the starting point. 
Task: Compute the total time needed until all trucks finish collecting and 
return, i.e., the maximum time among all trucks. 
 
iii) Given an n × m grid with an assassin A, guards (^ v < >), obstacles X, and 
empty cells (.) 
• Guards watch in a straight line in their facing direction until blocked by a 
wall/obstacle or another guard. All cells in their vision are unsafe. 
• The assassin starts at A and wants to reach the bottom-right cell (n-1, m-
1). 
• The assassin can move up/down/left/right on empty cells . only (not into 
obstacles, guards, or unsafe cells). 
• If the assassin starts in vision or the target is unsafe/blocked, they fail. 
Task: Determine if the assassin can safely reach the bottom-right cell. 
 
2.Interview 
 
Interview was held in virtual mode and consisted of two parts.  
i)Technical interview: 
Duration: 40 mins 
Started with self-introduction and projects that mentioned in my resume. I was 
asked to explain the features of my projects and how I have implemented 
them. Then, the interviewer asked me about the technologies I have 
knowledge and practical experience on. Then I was asked to code 2 questions 
in Notepad while sharing my screen 1. Using any programming language, 
merge all the files in a folder which couldn’t write the code completely but I 
explained the process and methods how it could be done  2. String reverse 
question with a little modification (reverse string word by word) which 
completely successfully. Then some questions on DBMS (on concurrency 
related to banking transactions and I was asked write 2 simple queries: finding 
duplicate entries but case in-sensitive and using group by and order by to find 
nth largest salary), Multithreading, OOPS (polymorphism and some other 
questions). Then the interviewer asked about my skills and knowledge on other 
tech such as AI, ML, Cloud etc,.. to which I replied that I have basic knowledge 
on AI did a course on it but haven’t done a project on. Then the interviewer 
finished by asking me do you have any questions.  
ii)HR interview: 
Duration: 55 mins 
Like the technical round, it also started with self-introduction and about my 
projects and the interviewer specifically asked many questions on how scalable 
is the project, the tech stack and the reason to use that and the team work and 
experience on working in the project and the interview slowly drifted towards 
my area of interests and expertise as I was asked questions like favourite 
subject and subject I hate and reason to choose CSE in CEG, the technology I 
am currently exploring and like to learn next, a technology that I don’t have in 
syllabus but I learnt it by myself and utilized it and liked it very much. Then 
some situation questions on a team project involving software development 
lifecycle questions and agile and other methods and my knowledge on that. I 
was also asked various other questions like how my team-mates describe me, 
my strengths and weaknesses, biggest achievement and failure, my inspiration, 
why Amex and why you are suitable for Amex etc,…                                    
My advise would be think before answering and answer the behavioural and 
situational questions via an example from your life experiences and elaborate 
on it and maintain a positive attitude even when you couldn’t answer 
completely and don’t feel worried about telling them that you haven’t learnt 
something yet. Be ambitious in your goals and sound confident. Try knowing 
about the company and role before the interview and it would be very useful.
