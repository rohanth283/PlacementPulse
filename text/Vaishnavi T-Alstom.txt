ALSTOM 
 
Alstom is a French multinational rolling stock manufacturer operating worldwide in rail transport 
markets, active in the fields of passenger transportation, signalling, and locomotives, with products 
including the AGV, TGV, Eurostar, Avelia and New Pendolino high-speed trains, in addition to 
suburban, regional and metro trains, as well as Citadis trams. 
 
Round 1: 
It was an online mcqs test around one hour. This round consists of 3 sections: Verbal Ability, 
Logical Reasoning, Quantitative ability. After completing this round , students were selected for Group 
Discussion  Round. 
 
Round 2 : 
It was a Group Disscussion round and it went for around 20 minutes. A team of  8 members 
participated in this round. Topics were  mostly about  current Affairs ,technologies and General topics. 
Topic was given by the panel. We  had 2-3 minutes for preparation. 
 
Round 3: 
It was a Technical Interview round around 30 minutes. 
1. Introduce yourself 
2. which subjects do you like the most ? and justify your answer. 
3. Projects:  Briefly described about projects in the resume. 
4. solve two programming questions. (where  two programs were easy level) 
1. Insertion and Searching in BST 
2. Check whether a Linked list having loop 
5. Questions based on Sql query. 
 
Round 4: 
It was an HR round around 15 minutes 
1)Introduce yourself 
2)What do you know about Alstom? 
3)Can you tell about the time when you demonstrated your leadership skills? 
4)what is your strength and development? 
 
Topics to be focused: 
➔ Data Structures 
➔ DBMS 
➔ OOPS 
 
Take away: 
Continuous Practice of problems from websites like geeksforgeeks, hackerearth, hackerrank will 
help you to crack the coding section in the online round.
